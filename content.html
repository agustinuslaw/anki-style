An der Kreuzung umdrehen,wenden
Turn around at an intersection (make a u-turn)
  1. Check that U turns are allowed in the area.
  2. Approach the intersection slowly.
  3. Signal left (you're turning across traffic).
  4. When the way is clear
  5. Turn left until going to opposite direction on the same road.
  6. It looks like a wide U shape.
